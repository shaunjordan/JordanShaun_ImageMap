Welcome to TRAPPIST-1

On February 23rd, 2017, astronomers announced the existence of seven earth-sized planets orbiting a red dwarf star just 40 light years from Earth. Current observations indicate three of the planets reside within the habitable zone, where life has the greatest chance to form. Though it is much too early to make any certain declarations, the discovery brings with it new enthusiasm for space exploration and study.
