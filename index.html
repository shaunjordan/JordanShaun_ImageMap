On February 23rd, 2017, astronomers announced the existence of seven earth-sized planets orbiting a red dwarf star just 40 light years from Earth. Current observations indicate that at least three of the planets may reside within the habitable zone, indicating the possibility life could form on the planets. Though it is much too early to make any certain declarations, the discovery brings with it new enthusiasm for space exploration and study.
